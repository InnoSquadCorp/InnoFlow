# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

InnoFlow is maintained as a professional open source project. Contributors and
maintainers are expected to keep discussion respectful, specific, and focused on
the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Assume good intent while asking for concrete evidence.
- Keep criticism tied to code, documentation, tests, or project direction.
- Respect maintainers' decisions on scope, timing, and release criteria.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Publicly sharing private contact information.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, pull requests, or discussions after a maintainer
  has asked to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close conversations, or restrict participation
when behavior violates this code. Enforcement decisions prioritize the safety and
health of the project community.
